What do you get when you cross a software engineer with a chef?

A kitchen full of bugs! Just kidding. Hi, I'm Ayoub Djellaoui, and I'm a software engineer who also happens to love cooking. I've always enjoyed the precise and logical thinking required in coding, but there's something about the creativity and improvisation of cooking that I find equally fulfilling.

On this website, I'm excited to share some of my favorite recipes and cooking tips with you. So come on in, pull up a seat, and let's get cooking (without the bugs, hopefully)!
